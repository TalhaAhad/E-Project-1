Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our facilities, memberships, and services.

What is your Operating hours?

Our facility is open Monday to Friday from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Do you offer Personal traning?

Yes, we have a team of certified personal trainers available for one-on-one sessions. You can book a consultation at the front desk or through our website.

Can i try class before joining?

Absolutely! We offer a complimentary trial class for new visitors. Please check our group exercise schedule and sign up at the front desk.

What is included in the Premium Pass membership?

The Premium Pass includes full access to the gym floor, unlimited group exercise classes, and full access to our swimming pool facilities.

Do you have changing rooms and lockers?

Yes, we provide clean and spacious changing rooms with secure lockers for your belongings. Please bring your own lock or purchase one at the front desk.
